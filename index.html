WiCS aims to bring together women and men who support the advancement of women in computer science, be it in academic, the tech industry, or elsewhere. We hope to support and encourage prospective computer science majors and provide direction and assistance to current computer science students.

Events

WiCS organizes all kinds of events during the year. We organize meetups for undergraduate and graduate students, including informal meet-and-greets and meetings with professors in the Computer Science department. We also frequently organize site visits, meaning that we take a small group of 15-20 members to a local NYC startup or company to speak with engineers. We also host networking events and assist in helping corporate sponsors host recruitment events for Columbia students.
